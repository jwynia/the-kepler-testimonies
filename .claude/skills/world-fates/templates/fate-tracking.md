## Usage Notes

### Power Level Scale

| Level | Scope | Examples |
|-------|-------|----------|
| 1-2 | Local | Village elder, small merchant, local hero |
| 3-4 | Regional | Town mayor, guild master, regional champion |
| 5-6 | National | Noble house, major faction, famous adventurer |
| 7-8 | Civilization | Kingdom ruler, legendary hero, archmage |
| 9-10 | World-shaping | Emperor, demigod, world-threatening power |

### Vulnerability Weights

- Known enemy: +1.0
- Active conflict: +1.5
- Exploitable flaw: +2.0
- Critical dependency: +1.0
- Destroying secret: +3.0

### Protection Weights

- Loyal ally: +1.0
- Fortified position: +2.0
- Divine/magical protection: +3.0
- Information network: +1.0
- Popular support: +2.0

### Tenure Modifiers

| Duration | Modifier |
|----------|----------|
| < 1 year | 0.5x |
| 1-5 years | 1.0x |
| 5-20 years | 1.5x |
| 20-50 years | 2.0x |
| 50+ years | 2.5x |

### Exposure Categories

| Category | Base Pressure | Examples |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| Combat | +10% | Battle, duel, assassination attempt |
| Intrigue | +5% | Political maneuvering, subtle threats |
| Betrayal | +15% | Ally turns, trust violated |
| Revelation | +20% | Secret exposed, truth revealed |
| Overreach | +10% | Hubris, overconfident action |
| Loss | +8% | Resource, ally, or territory lost |
| Challenge | +12% | Rival's claim, direct contest |
| Narrow Escape | +5% | Survived dangerous situation |
